## Day 2 — Mon 2026-06-08
**AM [EN]** Monday reminder: an ITIN is a tax number from the IRS. It is not your immigration status, and it is not work authorization. Anyone who needs to file U.S. taxes without being eligible for an SSN can have one. That includes a lot more people than most folks think.

**PM [ES]** ¿Comprar casa con ITIN? Sí se puede. Suelen ser préstamos de "portafolio": el banco lo conserva en vez de venderlo, y puede pedir un enganche más grande y una tasa algo más alta. Tus impuestos declarados ayudan mucho. https://itinlending.net/es #ITIN #hipoteca

## Day 3 — Tue 2026-06-09
**AM [EN]** Starting your credit from zero with an ITIN? The order of operations matters more than the product: 1) one reporting account (secured card or credit-builder loan), 2) autopay the full balance, 3) keep usage low, 4) wait. That's most of the game.

**PM [EN]** Heads up, because this one costs people real money: if a company wants an upfront fee for "guaranteed approval," walk away. Guaranteed approval is not a thing, and the fee is the scam. Honest lenders decide after they look at your file, not before.

**PM [ES]** Cuidado con el fraude del "CPN" o "número de crédito nuevo". Si alguien te ofrece un número para usar en vez de tu ITIN o SSN, eso es ilegal y te puede meter en problemas serios. Construir crédito honesto es más lento, pero es el único que aguanta. #ITIN #estafas #crédito

## Day 6 — Fri 2026-06-12
**AM [ES]** Recordatorio: solicitar tu ITIN con el IRS (Formulario W-7) es gratis hacerlo tú mismo. Si alguien te cobra cientos de dólares solo por "tramitarlo", muchas veces te está cobrando por un proceso gratuito. Un Agente Certificador (CAA) sí cobra, pero pregunta el precio antes.

**PM [EN]** Myth: "Renting doesn't help your credit." Not anymore. Some services report your on-time rent to the bureaus. For a thin ITIN file, that's one more positive account working in the background. Ask your landlord or a rent-reporting service. https://itinlending.net #ITIN
