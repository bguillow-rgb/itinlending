## Following strategy (compliant — no bots)

**Why no automation:** auto-follow scripts (e.g. 10 follows every 30 min) violate
X's platform-manipulation/spam policy and get brand-new accounts suspended fast.
We grow by hand, slowly, with real engagement.

**Safe pace for a new account:**
- Week 1: ~10–20 follows/day. Week 2+: up to ~30–40/day. Never burst.
- For every few follows, also like or leave a genuine reply (the `itin-social`
  skill writes these). Following without engaging looks botty.
- Do **not** follow/unfollow churn. Keep follows you mean.
- Post the pinned intro + a few real posts BEFORE following anyone, so your
  profile doesn't look empty/spammy when people get the follow notification.

**Verify every handle in-app before following** (our own skill rule — a wrong @
is embarrassing). The list below is target *organizations/categories*, not
confirmed handles.
