# Off-page outreach target list — ITIN sites (2026-06-13)

Compiled for the three Timberline ITIN sites. Goal: earn **legitimate** links and
brand mentions from third-party "best of" listicles, comparison posts, and
community threads where our competitors already appear and we don't (co-citation
+ listicle outreach, per playbook Step 9).

## The hard boundary (read first)

**Automated link placement = spam = penalties.** I do not, and will not, auto-post
links to forums, comments, directories, or blog networks. What is safely automatable
is everything *around* earning links — target discovery (this list), monitoring,
linkable-asset creation, and indexing. **Sending any outreach email, submitting any
form, or posting to any community requires your explicit go-ahead each time** — I'll
draft, you approve and send (or tell me to send on your behalf).

Legend — **Impact**: H/M/L expected link/citation value. **Effort**: how hard to land.

## Tier 3 — Linkable assets to BUILD (so links come to us)

Assets earn links passively — the highest-leverage automatable off-page work. I can
generate/scaffold these in-repo:

1. **Free ITIN credit-score eligibility / readiness tool** (calculator) — strongest
   linkable asset; "tools" earn links far above articles. Candidate next build.
2. **Original data drop** — e.g. an "ITIN borrower payment-discipline" explainer built
   around the Experian white-paper stats we already cite, packaged as a citable chart.
   Writers covering the space link to original framing.
3. **Bilingual resource hub** — a single EN+ES "ITIN credit & lending resource" page
   pitchable to immigrant-services orgs and .edu/.org community sites for legitimate links.
