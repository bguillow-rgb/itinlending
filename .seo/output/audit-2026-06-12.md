## itincreditscore.com — the leader, push it to page 2

**Why it's winning:** broadest query footprint (78), most impressions (729), and the
tightest topical focus ("check credit score with ITIN" is a clear, lower-competition
intent). Several queries already pos 32–43 — one good push from page 3.

Actionable, ranked by leverage:

1. **[HIGH] Concentrate authority on `/check-credit-score-with-itin`.** It owns the
   single biggest query — `how to check credit score with itin` = **183 impr but pos
   70**. Cannibalizing legacy URLs are already redirected in `astro.config.mjs`
   (`/credit-reports-with-itin`, `/start-building-now`) — good; just monitor GSC
   consolidates over the next 2–4 weeks. Then: add 2–3 internal links INTO this page
   from the homepage + pillar + every relevant article using descriptive anchors
   ("how to check your credit score with an ITIN"), and make sure it's the deepest,
   most complete answer on the site. *Impact: high. Time: 2–4 wks to move.*
2. **[HIGH] Add a comparison table** to that page: bureau-by-bureau (Experian /
   TransUnion / Equifax) ITIN support + AnnualCreditReport.com behavior. Three of the
   gap queries are bureau-specific (`transunion credit report itin` pos 32,
   `annualcreditreport.com itin` pos 38, `experian credit report with itin`). A table
   is what AI Overviews and rich results pull. *Impact: high. Time: 2–4 wks.*
3. **[MED] Targeted clusters for the pos-32–43 queries** — these are striking-range
   for a young site. Ensure each has an atomic H2 (question-phrased) answering it in
   ~150 words with a cited stat. *Impact: med. Time: 4–8 wks.*

## itinlending.net — laggard, hardest niche

1. **[HIGH] Reset expectations + go long-tail.** "itin loans", "itin mortgage" are
   bid-heavy commercial head terms dominated by aged lenders — pos 82–97 means it's
   not in the running for those yet. Pivot the content queue to specific, low-comp
   long-tails: "itin mortgage requirements", "itin personal loan bad credit",
   "[state] itin home loan", "itin auto loan no ssn". Win the long tail first, let
   authority compound up to the heads. *Impact: high. Time: 8–12 wks.*
2. **[MED] Internal-link the money pages from articles.** With 9 articles and 16
   pages, ensure every research article links to the relevant money page
   (loans/mortgage/auto) with descriptive anchors — concentrates what little authority
   exists onto the converting pages. *Impact: med. Time: 1–2 wks.*
3. **[LOW] It will lag the other two for months** — that's the niche, not a defect.
   Don't over-invest here until creditscore proves the playbook.
