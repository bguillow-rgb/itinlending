Our Lender & Marketing Partners

Last updated July 12, 2026

When you submit a request on ITIN Lending, you ask to be matched with lenders and financing partners who work with ITIN holders. This page lists the partners, and the categories of partners, that we may share your request with and that you give consent to be contacted by. We keep this list current; it may change as we add or remove partners, so check back for the latest roster or contact us for the list in effect on any given date.

Who may contact you

By submitting our form and checking the consent box, you agree that ITIN Lending and the following partners may contact you at the phone number and email you provided, including by autodialed or prerecorded calls and text messages, about loan and financing options:

  • Engine by MoneyLion and the banks, lenders, and financial-service providers in its offer marketplace, which returns personal-loan, auto, mortgage, and credit-card offers.
  • Lending marketplaces and lead-distribution partners we work with to match your request with lenders (for example, ping-post mortgage and auto networks). These partners, in turn, may present your request to lenders in their own networks.
  • Individual lenders and financing companies that serve ITIN borrowers across personal, auto, mortgage, and business loans, whom we may introduce you to directly by referral.

How to stop contact or opt out

You can withdraw consent at any time. Ask any caller or texter to stop and they must; reply STOP to any text message; or email us at bguillow@gmail.com to be removed from further outreach. If you are a California resident, you can also exercise your right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information on our Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information page. See our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use for the full picture.
